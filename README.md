# Signal & Shift — AI Business Intelligence Newsletter

🔗 Live Site: https://sagar-alande.github.io/newsletter-site/

---

## 📌 Overview

**Signal & Shift** is a premium, editorial-style AI newsletter designed to analyze how artificial intelligence is being applied in real-world business environments.

Instead of focusing on model releases or technical hype, this project examines how AI is actively transforming:

- operational workflows  
- decision-making systems  
- business efficiency and scalability  

This issue covers **three recent commercial AI developments (April 2026)** across:

- Healthcare  
- Financial Services  
- Retail & Supply Chain  

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to demonstrate how AI moves beyond prediction into **optimization and execution**.

Each section answers:
- What changed?
- Why does it matter?
- How does it impact business decisions?

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

> The real value of AI is not in prediction alone — it is in how predictions improve decisions and optimize systems at scale.
